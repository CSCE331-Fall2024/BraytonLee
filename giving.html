Giving Back

Public service has always been one of my foremost callings. I have been privileged to be able to serve my community as a firefighter since 2013. By answering calls for service, I am able to help my friends and neighbors in times of need, and in my own small way, try to make things better for them. I have also learned how to quickly assess situations and confidently make decisions based on my training and experience.

I have developed a wide skill set, from fire suppression to hazardous materials and water rescue. I am amazed at how often these diverse skills can be applied in every situation. I heartily encourage everyone to find their own outlet where they can give back and, in some way, try to make the world a better place.
